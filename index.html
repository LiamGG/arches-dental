Arches British Dental Practice

Established in 2006, we have built a reputation of offering gentle dental care in a relaxed setting.

Our multi-national patient base includes full-time residents, intermittent visitors, and one-off attenders including holiday-makers.
We speak English, Spanish, and German. The building is also wheelchair accessible.

The services we provide include general, preventive and cosmetic dental treatments, as well as implants. Click here to see a more complete list of our services.

We also offer interest-free finance over 6 months, and written estimates of costs and treatments. Please contact us if you have any questions.
